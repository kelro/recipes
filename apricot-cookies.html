Apricot Cookies

Ingredients
  • 1 package (8 oz) cream cheese
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp milk

Apricot Filling

  • 2 packages dried apricots
  • 1 cup sugar

Glaze

  • ¾ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tbsp water
Procedure
  1. Mix cream cheese, butter, flour, salt, vinegar, and milk. Refrigerate 2–3 hours.
  2. Boil apricots with sugar until semi-soft. Cool.
  3. Roll dough and cut with diamond-shaped cutter.
  4. Add filling, fold corners over center.
  5. Bake at 400°F for about 13 minutes.
  6. Cool completely and glaze.
