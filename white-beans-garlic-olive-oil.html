White Beans with Garlic and Olive Oil

A Mediterranean bean dish with olive oil and garlic.

Ingredients
  • 1 pound dried white beans
  • 8 cups water (for soaking)
  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt to taste
Procedure
  1. Soak beans overnight and cook until tender.
  2. Warm olive oil and gently sauté garlic.
  3. Toss beans with garlic oil and season with salt.
