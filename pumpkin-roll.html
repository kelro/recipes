Pumpkin Roll

From Elizabeth Kelley

Desserts Bake: 375°F • 15 min Pan: 15" x 10" x 1" jelly roll

Ingredients
Measure everything first, then mix.

Dry Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Wet Ingredients

  • 3 eggs (beat on high for 5 minutes)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup pumpkin
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup finely chopped nuts

Topping

  • 1/4 cup finely chopped nuts
  • Powdered sugar (for the towel)

Filling

  • 6 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
Procedure
Bake, roll warm, cool, fill, chill.
  1. Prep dry: Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Beat eggs: Beat the eggs on high for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix wet: Add sugar, pumpkin, lemon juice, and 1 cup finely chopped nuts to the eggs; mix to combine.
  4. Combine: Fold the dry mixture into the pumpkin mixture.
  5. Pan: Spread batter into a greased and floured 15" x 10" x 1" jelly roll pan.
  6. Top: Sprinkle the top with 1/4 cup finely chopped nuts.
  7. Bake: Bake at 375°F for 15 minutes.
  8. Roll warm: Turn the cake out onto a towel sprinkled with powdered sugar. Roll the towel and cake together while warm.
  9. Cool: Let the rolled cake cool completely.
  10. Make filling: Beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, butter, and vanilla until smooth.
  11. Fill + roll: Unroll the cooled cake, spread filling evenly, then roll back up.
  12. Chill: Chill before slicing and serving.
Notes: Rolling the cake while it’s warm helps prevent cracking later.
