Green Smoothie

A refreshing, nutrient-dense smoothie with fruit and leafy greens.

Ingredients
For the green smoothie
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup frozen berries
  • 2 cups leafy greens (spinach or kale)
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • ½ cup water (as needed for blending)
Procedure
Blend until smooth
  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until completely smooth, adding water as needed to reach desired consistency.
  3. Serve immediately.
Notes: Drink fresh for best flavor and nutrition.

Nutrition (per serving):
• 164 calories
• 6 g fat
• 145 mg sodium
• 5 g fiber
• 2 g protein
