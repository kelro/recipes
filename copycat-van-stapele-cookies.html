Copycat Van Stapele Cookies (Amsterdam Cookies)

Rich, decadent dark chocolate cookies with milk chocolate chips and a white chocolate center.

Desserts Baked ~90 minutes

Ingredients
For 26 cookies
  • 1½ cups (180 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (226 g) unsalted butter
  • ¾ cup (88.5 g) dark cocoa powder
  • ¾ cup (160 g) packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup (100 g) granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup (180 g) milk chocolate chips
  • 2.6 oz white chocolate, broken into 26 rectangular pieces (about 0.1 oz each)
Procedure
Prepare and bake
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 °F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter with the cocoa powder, stirring until smooth and glossy. Remove from heat and allow to cool to room temperature.
  4. In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, combine the cooled butter mixture with the brown sugar and granulated sugar. Mix on medium speed until smooth, about 2 minutes.
  5. Add the eggs and vanilla extract and mix until fully combined.
  6. With the mixer on low speed, add the flour mixture until just combined. Stir in the milk chocolate chips.
  7. Refrigerate the dough for 1 hour to firm up.
  8. Using a 1-inch cookie scoop, portion 52 balls of dough. Two balls will form one cookie.
  9. Flatten one ball slightly, place a piece of white chocolate in the center, top with a second ball, and seal the edges. Repeat to make 26 cookies.
  10. Place cookies on the baking sheet with plenty of space between them. Bake for about 10 minutes.
Notes: These cookies spread significantly, so leave ample space between them. Slight underbaking helps preserve the soft center and molten white chocolate filling.
