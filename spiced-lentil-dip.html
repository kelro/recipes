Spiced Lentil Dip

A warm, hummus-like lentil dip with spices — great with pita or fresh bread.

Appetizer Stovetop / Oven ~30 minutes

Ingredients
Ingredients for spiced lentil dip
  • 1 (15-oz) can lentils or 1½ cups cooked lentils
  • 1 cup low-sodium vegetable stock
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 small onions, chopped
  • ¼ teaspoon powdered ginger or 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Salt and paprika, to taste
Procedure
Step-by-step instructions
  1. Cook the onion in the olive oil for about 5 minutes over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the flour, then add 1 cup of vegetable stock.
  3. Cook and stir over medium heat until the mixture comes to a boil and thickens slightly.
  4. Combine the drained lentils with the sauce in the pan.
  5. Season with ginger, chili powder, lemon juice, salt, and paprika.
  6. Place in an oven-proof dish and keep warm in a 300 °F oven until ready to serve.
Notes: Per the original source, this recipe yields **approximately 1,340 calories per batch** with **70 g protein and 32 g fat** (including 3 g saturated and 16 g polyunsaturated fat). For **4 servings**, this equates to **about 340 calories per serving**. Serve warm with pita bread or toasted sourdough.
