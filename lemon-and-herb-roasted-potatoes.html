Lemon and Herb Roasted Potatoes

Classic roasted potatoes with lemon, garlic, oregano, and fresh mint — a versatile side dish.

Side Dishes Baked ~1 hr 15 mins

Ingredients
For the lemon and herb roasted potatoes
  • 5 pounds potatoes, scrubbed and cut into thin wedges
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¾ cup olive oil
  • ½ cup good-quality vegetable broth
  • ½ cup water
  • ¼ cup fresh-squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 teaspoons sea salt, plus more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, plus more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh mint
Procedure
Oven roasting steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 450 °F and lightly oil a large baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, stir the potatoes, garlic, olive oil, broth, water, lemon juice, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  3. Pour the potato mixture into the prepared baking dish.
  4. Roast in the preheated oven until the potatoes start to brown, about 40 minutes.
  5. Add oregano and mint, stirring to combine. If the potatoes look dry, pour another ½ cup broth over them.
  6. Return to the oven and bake about 30 minutes longer.
  7. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
Notes: These roasted potatoes are a staple comfort side in Greek cuisine; keeping the skin on increases fiber and micronutrients.
