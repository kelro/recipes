Golden Spice Wellness Drink

A warming, anti-inflammatory wellness drink supporting digestion, circulation, and metabolic health.

Ingredients
Optional ingredients enhance absorption and flavor.
  • 1 cup hot water or unsweetened almond milk
  • ½ teaspoon Ceylon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
  • Small pinch black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon coconut or MCT oil (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon raw honey (optional)
  • Dash cayenne pepper (optional)
Procedure
Warm, steep, and sip slowly.
  1. Warm the water or almond milk; do not boil.
  2. Add cinnamon, turmeric, ginger, and black pepper.
  3. Stir well and let steep for 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Add coconut oil and lemon juice if using; stir until blended.
  5. Allow to cool slightly, then add honey if desired.
  6. Sip slowly while warm.
Notes: Best consumed in the morning on an empty stomach or in the evening 1–2 hours before bed. Stir before each sip.
