Pumpkin Pie

Classic recipe from Libby’s Pumpkin

Ingredients
For one 9-inch deep-dish pie.
  • 1 (9-inch) homemade or store-bought deep-dish pie crust, unbaked
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 (15-oz) can pumpkin purée (Libby’s)
  • 1 (12-oz) can evaporated milk
Procedure
Two-stage bake for best texture.
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. In a large bowl, beat eggs and pumpkin together.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger, and cloves. Add to pumpkin mixture.
  4. Gradually stir in the evaporated milk until smooth.
  5. Carefully pour filling into the unbaked pie crust.
  6. Bake at 425°F for 15 minutes.
  7. Reduce oven temperature to 350°F and bake an additional 40–50 minutes, or until set.
  8. Pie is done when the center jiggles just slightly, or when a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
  9. Cool on a wire rack before serving.
Tip: The pie will continue to set as it cools. Chill before slicing for the cleanest cuts.
