Avocado & Tuna Tomato Salad Stack

A fresh, layered salad with creamy avocado, tuna, and tomato.

Ingredients
Serves 2
  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1 can (5 oz) tuna (albacore or yellowfin), drained
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice, divided
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely diced
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and diced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt, to taste
Procedure
Assemble just before serving
  1. Drain the tuna and place in a bowl. Add mayonnaise, cilantro, and 1 tablespoon of the lime juice. Season with salt and mix gently.
  2. In a separate bowl, toss the diced avocado with 1 tablespoon of the lime juice to prevent browning.
  3. In another bowl, combine tomatoes, red onion, jalapeño, remaining lime juice, garlic powder, and salt.
  4. To assemble, layer avocado on a plate, top with the tuna mixture, then finish with the tomato mixture.
  5. Serve immediately while fresh.
Notes: Best served immediately. This dish pairs well with crusty bread or as a light protein-rich meal on its own.
