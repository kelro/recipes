Ikaria-Style Roasted Vegetables

Simple roasted seasonal vegetables with olive oil and herbs.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups chopped seasonal vegetables (zucchini, onion, peppers)
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt to taste
Procedure
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Toss vegetables with olive oil, oregano, and salt.
  3. Roast 30–35 minutes until tender and lightly browned.
