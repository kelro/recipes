Lentil Soup (Ikaria & Sardinia)

A simple lentil soup inspired by Blue Zones regions

Ingredients
Brightened with lemon and olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 cup lentils, rinsed
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)
Procedure
Simmer until tender, finish with lemon
  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, garlic, and carrot; sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add lentils, broth, bay leaf, and oregano.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 25–30 minutes, until lentils are tender.
  5. Remove bay leaf. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Stir in lemon juice just before serving.
  7. Garnish with parsley if desired.
