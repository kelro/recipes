Cornbread & Cream Corn Casserole

From Tony Heimbach

Ingredients
Simple pantry ingredients.
  • 1 box cornbread mix (Jiffy plain)
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 2 cans cream corn
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil (e.g., Wesson)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup grated cheese (your preference)
Procedure
Mix, bake, and test for doneness.
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cornbread mix, garlic powder, eggs, cream corn, oil, salt, and cheese.
  3. Cheese option: You can mix 1/2 cup of the cheese into the batter and reserve the rest for topping.
  4. Pour mixture into the prepared baking dish.
  5. If topping with cheese, bake for a while first, then add remaining cheese on top and continue baking.
  6. Bake for approximately 60 minutes.
  7. Test for doneness by gently shaking the dish — the center should be set.
Notes: Baking times may vary slightly depending on dish depth. If the top browns too quickly, loosely cover with foil.
