Okinawan Stir-Fried Vegetables

Lightly cooked vegetables in Okinawan style.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (cabbage, carrots, onions)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
Procedure
  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add vegetables and stir-fry briefly until just tender.
  3. Season with salt and serve immediately.
