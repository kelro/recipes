Teriyaki Tofu Don

A crispy tofu bowl with savory teriyaki sauce served over rice.

Main Dishes Stovetop Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients
Crispy tofu with teriyaki flavor
  • 1/4 cup (60 ml) canola oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 16 oz (450 g) firm tofu, cubed
  • 1/4 cup (30 g) potato starch or cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1/3 cup (100 ml) teriyaki sauce
  • 1½ cups (300 g) cooked Japanese rice
  • Japanese mayonnaise (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons white sesame seeds
  • Daikon microgreens or 1 small scallion, chopped, for garnish
Procedure
Pan-fry tofu and finish with savory teriyaki
  1. Add 2 tablespoons of canola oil and the chopped onion to a skillet. Sauté over medium heat until the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Combine tofu and potato starch in a bowl; toss until tofu is fully coated.
  3. In the same skillet, heat remaining canola oil. Add tofu and sauté until golden and crisp, about 5 minutes.
  4. Drizzle toasted sesame oil over the tofu, then add the cooked onion back to the pan. Reduce heat to low and pour in the teriyaki sauce; mix to coat everything evenly.
  5. Divide cooked Japanese rice into bowls. Top with the tofu mixture.
  6. Drizzle Japanese mayonnaise over top if using, and garnish with sesame seeds and microgreens or scallions.
