A bean-forward longevity stew from Ikaria.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup dried white beans
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried herbs
  • Salt to taste
Procedure
  1. Cook beans until tender.
  2. Sauté onion in olive oil.
  3. Add herbs and beans, simmer briefly.
