Lentils with Herbs

Simple Mediterranean lentils

Ingredients
  • 1 cup dried brown lentils
  • 3 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh parsley and thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure
  1. Simmer lentils in water until tender.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
  3. Combine lentils and herbs. Season and serve.
