Sweet Potato Casserole

A classic holiday casserole with a pecan brown sugar topping.

Desserts Oven 350°F · ~35 minutes

Ingredients
Sweet potato base and pecan topping

Sweet Potato Mixture

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, cooked and mashed
  • 1 egg
  • 1 stick butter, melted
  • 1/3 cup evaporated milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla

Topping

  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 2 cups chopped pecans
  • 2/3 cup flour
  • 2/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
Procedure
Bake until set and golden
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix all sweet potato ingredients until smooth.
  3. Pour mixture into a greased baking dish.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix all topping ingredients until crumbly.
  5. Spread topping evenly over the sweet potato mixture.
  6. Bake for about 35 minutes, or until set and not soggy.
