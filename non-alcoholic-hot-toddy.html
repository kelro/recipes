Non-Alcoholic Hot Toddy

A soothing warm drink with honey, lemon, and spices — comforting for cold evenings or a scratchy throat.

Beverages Stovetop ~10 minutes

Ingredients
For one warm drink
  • ¾ cup hot water or hot herbal tea (ginger or chamomile work well)
  • 2–3 teaspoons honey, to taste
  • 2–3 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, to taste
  • 1 slice lemon
  • Optional: 1 cinnamon stick or a pinch of ground cloves
Procedure
Stir and sip
  1. Heat water or herbal tea until hot but not boiling.
  2. Pour into a mug and add honey and lemon juice.
  3. Stir until the honey dissolves completely.
  4. Add a lemon slice and optional cinnamon stick or cloves.
  5. Sip slowly while warm.
Notes: This alcohol-free hot toddy is gentle and suitable any time of day. Honey helps coat and soothe the throat, while lemon adds brightness and a small vitamin C boost. Herbal teas like ginger or chamomile add additional calming properties.
