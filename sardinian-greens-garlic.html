Sardinian Greens with Garlic

Simple sautéed greens from Sardinia.

Ingredients
  • 1 pound leafy greens (chard or dandelion greens)
  • 2 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
Procedure
  1. Blanch greens briefly and drain.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add greens and cook until tender. Season with salt.
