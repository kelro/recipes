Ingredients
Basic, adaptable curry sauce.
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1–2 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2–3 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
Procedure
Sauté, simmer, and blend if desired.
  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, and grated ginger. Cook until the onion is soft and translucent, about 5–7 minutes.
  3. Stir in the curry powder and cook for 1–2 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and broth; stir to combine.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 15–20 minutes, until slightly thickened.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and let cool slightly. Blend with an immersion blender or standard blender until smooth, if desired.
  8. Serve over rice or with chicken, beef, pork, tofu, or vegetables.
Variations: Substitute coconut milk for broth, add fresh herbs like cilantro or basil, or include spices such as cumin, turmeric, or coriander to customize the flavor.
