Classic Chicken Salad

A simple, creamy chicken salad with celery, onion, and optional mix-ins.

Ingredients
Classic base with optional additions
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded or diced
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise (or Greek yogurt for a lighter option)
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 3/4 teaspoon lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, or rice vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
  • 1/2 cup celery, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans or almonds (optional)
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries or halved grapes (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
Procedure
Mix, chill, and serve
  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Add the cooked chicken, celery, red onion, nuts (if using), dried cranberries or grapes, and parsley. Stir until well combined.
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  5. Serve chilled on sandwiches, wraps, lettuce cups, or as a side dish.
