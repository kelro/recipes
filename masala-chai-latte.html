Masala Chai Latte

A warm, spiced tea simmered with milk and aromatic whole spices.

Beverages Stovetop Makes 5 cups

Ingredients
Whole spices, tea, and milk
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cinnamon stick (about 2 inches)
  • 6 fennel seeds
  • 6 whole cloves
  • 6 cardamom seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon powdered masala
  • 5 teaspoons loose black tea or tea leaves
  • 3/4 cup condensed milk or half & half
  • 2 1/4 cups whole milk
  • Honey, to taste (optional)
Procedure
Simmer slowly to develop flavor
  1. Lightly grind the cinnamon, fennel seeds, cloves, and cardamom seeds.
  2. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil in a saucepan. Add the ground spices and powdered masala.
  3. Allow the spiced water to boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the tea leaves to the boiling water and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in the condensed milk (or half & half) and whole milk.
  6. Bring the mixture back to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until a light brown film forms on top.
  7. Remove from heat. Sweeten with honey if desired and strain before serving.
Notes

For a stronger tea flavor, eliminate the final 2 cups of whole milk.
