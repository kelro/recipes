Okinawan Cream of Mushroom Soup

A light, dairy-free mushroom soup inspired by Okinawan cuisine.

Ingredients
Simple Okinawan flavors
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 cups unsweetened soy milk
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • Salt to taste
Procedure
Gentle heat preserves flavor
  1. In a large pot, sauté onion in vegetable oil with a pinch of salt until soft, about 5–6 minutes.
  2. Add mushrooms and continue sautéing until softened.
  3. Add ginger, soy milk, and bay leaf; bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Simmer for 3 minutes.
  5. Turn off heat, remove bay leaf, and stir in miso before serving.
