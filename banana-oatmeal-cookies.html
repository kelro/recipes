Banana Oatmeal Cookies

Naturally sweet cookies made with ripe bananas and wholesome oats — a simple plant-based treat.

Snacks Baked ~30 minutes

Ingredients
For the banana oatmeal cookies
  • 2 cups old fashioned oats
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1½ teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
  • 4 medium ripe bananas
  • ⅓ cup chopped walnuts
Procedure
Bake the wholesome cookies
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 °F and line a baking sheet with parchment or spray with non-stick spray.
  2. Use a high-power blender to process the oats to the consistency of flour, then pour into a mixing bowl and add baking soda and spices.
  3. Blend the bananas until completely smooth, then add to the oat mixture along with the walnuts and mix until combined.
  4. Drop 2-tablespoon balls of dough onto the baking sheet, spacing well. Dip the scoop or spoon into water to keep the dough from sticking, and use lightly moistened fingers to flatten each cookie.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes.
  6. Allow the cookies to cool on a wire rack, then store in an airtight container.
Notes: These cookies are naturally sweetened with ripe bananas and require no added sugar, making them a wholesome snack or breakfast on the go. Adjust the spices to taste, and feel free to swap walnuts for other nuts or dried fruit.
