Ikaria-Style Chickpeas

Slow-cooked chickpeas with olive oil and herbs from Ikaria.

Ingredients
  • 1 pound dried chickpeas
  • 8 cups water (for soaking)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt to taste
Procedure
  1. Soak chickpeas overnight in water. Drain.
  2. Simmer chickpeas in fresh water until tender, about 90 minutes.
  3. Add onion, olive oil, garlic, bay leaf, and oregano.
  4. Simmer gently 30 minutes more. Season with salt.
