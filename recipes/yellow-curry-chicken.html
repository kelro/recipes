Yellow Curry Chicken

Ingredients
Homemade yellow curry powder + quick coconut curry.

Dry Spice Blend (Yellow Curry Powder)

  • 1 tbsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon (or Ceylon)
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp chili powder or crushed red pepper flakes
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp fennel seed, crushed

Yellow Curry Sauce

  • 2 tbsp oil (vegetable or coconut oil)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced (or 1 tsp garlic powder)
  • 1 lb chicken (thigh or breast), cubed
  • 1 tbsp yellow curry powder (from above)
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 tsp salt (to taste)
  • Optional: 1 tsp sugar
  • Optional: 1 tsp fish sauce or soy sauce
  • Optional: 1 tsp flour or cornstarch (for thickening)
Procedure
Bloom the spices, then simmer until rich and glossy.
  1. Sauté onion in oil over medium heat until soft (3–5 minutes).
  2. Add garlic and 1 tbsp yellow curry powder. Stir for 30 seconds to bloom the spices.
  3. Add chicken. Stir to coat, then cook until browned on the outside (not fully cooked yet).
  4. Pour in coconut milk. Stir and bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Cover and simmer 15–20 minutes, until chicken is cooked through and sauce thickens.
  6. Taste and adjust with salt. Optional: add sugar and/or fish sauce/soy sauce to balance.
  7. If you want it thicker, whisk in a small amount of flour or cornstarch (slurry works best) and simmer briefly.
Optional add-ins: potatoes, carrots, peas, bell peppers. Finish with fresh basil or cilantro.
Serve with: steamed jasmine or basmati rice; naan or flatbread.
