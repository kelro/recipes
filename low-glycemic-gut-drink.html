Low-Glycemic Gut Drink

A diabetes-focused gut drink formulated to improve glucose handling and support beneficial bacteria.

Ingredients
Use unsweetened products only to avoid glucose spikes.
  • 1/2 cup plain, unsweetened kefir or unsweetened coconut kefir
  • 1 teaspoon green banana flour or resistant starch (potato starch)
  • 1/2 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder, matcha powder, or blueberry powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon psyllium husk powder
  • Optional: pinch of Ceylon cinnamon or 1 tsp apple cider vinegar (or both)
Procedure
Drink shortly before meals for best glucose response.
  1. Add the kefir to a glass or small blender cup.
  2. Stir in the green banana flour or resistant starch until smooth.
  3. Add the chosen polyphenol powder and mix thoroughly.
  4. Stir in the psyllium husk powder and optional cinnamon.
  5. Mix well and drink promptly.
Notes: Drink 10 to 15 minutes before your largest carbohydrate-containing meal. Start with half the resistant starch amount for the first week to reduce gas and bloating. Avoid sweeteners, fruit juice, or sweetened kefir, as these negate the low-glycemic benefit.
