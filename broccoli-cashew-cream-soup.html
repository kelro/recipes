Broccoli Cashew Cream Soup

A creamy, dairy-free soup using cashews for richness.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked 2 hours
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups broccoli florets, chopped
  • 5–6 cups vegetable broth or water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure
  1. Drain soaked cashews.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in broth until softened.
  3. Add broccoli and broth; simmer 15–20 minutes.
  4. Add cashews and blend until smooth.
  5. Season and warm gently before serving.
