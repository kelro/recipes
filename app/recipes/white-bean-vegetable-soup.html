White Bean Vegetable Soup

A comforting, protein-rich soup built around white beans and seasonal vegetables.

Ingredients
Simple and nourishing
  • 1 cup dried white beans, soaked overnight
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup potatoes or squash, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 6–8 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure
Slow simmer develops depth
  1. Drain soaked beans and rinse thoroughly.
  2. Sauté onion, carrots, celery, and garlic in a large pot using a splash of broth until softened.
  3. Add beans, potatoes or squash, tomatoes, thyme, bay leaf, and broth.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 60–90 minutes until beans are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and remove bay leaf before serving.
