Protein Smoothie

A thick, chocolatey smoothie with bananas, cherries, peanut butter, and Greek yogurt.

Ingredients
For the protein smoothie
  • 1½ cups unsweetened vanilla soymilk
  • 2 medium frozen bananas, sliced (about 1½ cups)
  • 1 cup frozen pitted dark cherries
  • ½ cup reduced-fat plain strained (Greek-style) yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons natural peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
Procedure
Blend until smooth
  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until completely smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
Notes: For a thinner smoothie, add a splash of extra soymilk. For added protein, you can include a scoop of unflavored or chocolate protein powder.
