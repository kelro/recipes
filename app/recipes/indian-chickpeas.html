Indian Chickpeas

Bright and flavorful chickpeas simmered with turmeric, ginger, mint, tomatoes, and lemon.

Ingredients
Ingredients for Indian-style chickpeas
  • 1 (16-oz) can chickpeas or 1 pound chickpeas, cooked
  • ½ cup low-sodium vegetable stock
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 4 tablespoons oil
  • 1½ teaspoons turmeric
  • ¾ tablespoon fresh ginger root (or ¼ teaspoon powdered ginger)
  • 6 fresh mint leaves
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
Procedure
Step-by-step Indian Chickpeas preparation
  1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat and cook the onions until softened.
  2. Add turmeric, fresh or powdered ginger, and mint leaves; stir briefly to release aromatics.
  3. Add the chickpeas, chili powder, vegetable stock, and chopped tomatoes.
  4. Simmer uncovered for 10–12 minutes until the tomatoes break down and flavors meld.
  5. Add salt to taste and stir well.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in the lemon juice.
Notes: Serve warm over rice or alongside flatbread. Adjust chili powder for mild or spicy heat.
