Olivia’s Old South Chicken Salad

A classic Southern-style chicken salad with shredded chicken, eggs, and sweet pickle relish.

Main Dishes No-Cook ~15 minutes

Ingredients
For Olivia’s Old South chicken salad
  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons celery, finely minced
  • ⅔ cup Duke’s mayonnaise
  • ⅛ teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 hard boiled eggs, peeled and diced
  • 3 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  • 1 tablespoon pickle juice
Procedure
Mix and chill
  1. Use a mixer with a paddle attachment or shred by hand to prepare the chicken.
  2. Finely mince the celery so it blends smoothly into the salad.
  3. In a large bowl, combine chicken, celery, eggs, sweet pickle relish, mayonnaise, onion powder, and pickle juice; stir until combined.
  4. Refrigerate for 1–2 hours in a covered dish to let the flavors meld.
  5. Serve on bread, croissants, crackers, or over salad greens.
