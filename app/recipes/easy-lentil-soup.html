Easy Lentil Soup

Everyday lentil soup with vegetables and herbs

Ingredients
Hearty and simple
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup lentils, rinsed
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)
Procedure
Sauté aromatics, then simmer lentils until tender
  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, carrots, and celery; sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and cook 1 minute more.
  4. Stir in lentils, broth, cumin, and thyme.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until lentils are tender, about 25–30 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve warm, garnished with parsley if desired.
