Basil Pesto

A classic Italian sauce made with fresh basil, garlic, nuts, olive oil, and Parmesan.

Sauces Food Processor 10 minutes

Ingredients
For classic basil pesto
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • ½ cup pine nuts (or walnuts or almonds)
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
Procedure
Blend until smooth
  1. Lightly toast the pine nuts in a dry pan over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until golden. Allow to cool.
  2. In a food processor, combine the cooled nuts, basil, and garlic. Process until finely chopped.
  3. With the processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the mixture is smooth and emulsified.
  4. Stir in the Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Use immediately or store in a tightly sealed container in the refrigerator or freezer.
Notes: Pesto is highly adaptable. Try substituting parsley or spinach for basil, walnuts or almonds for pine nuts, or pecorino cheese for Parmesan. To prevent browning during storage, pour a thin layer of olive oil over the surface before sealing.
