Mediterranean Grain Bowl

A nutrient-dense bowl with quinoa, roasted sweet potatoes, chickpeas, and greens.

Ingredients
  • 4 cups kale, stems removed
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled & 1-inch cubed
  • Extra-virgin olive oil spray
  • 1½ cups cooked chickpeas (or 1 can, drained)
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp coriander
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • ¼ tsp smoked paprika
  • Garlic powder (¼ tsp)
  • Cayenne (¼ tsp, optional)
  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa, rinsed & drained
  • 1¾ cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Pinch fresh-ground black pepper
  • ½ cup Lemon Tahini Herb Sauce
  • 2–3 tbsp sunflower seeds (for garnish)
  • Dill for garnish
Procedure
  1. Cut or tear kale into bite-size pieces. Add lemon juice and salt; massage for about 1 minute to soften.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Spread sweet potatoes on one half and mist with olive oil; sprinkle with salt.
  4. Place drained chickpeas on the other half. Sprinkle turmeric, coriander, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne over chickpeas; toss to coat.
  5. Roast sweet potatoes and chickpeas 15 minutes. Flip and continue roasting another 15 minutes until potatoes are tender and chickpeas are golden.
  6. Meanwhile cook quinoa: dry-sauté rinsed quinoa 3 minutes, then add water, turmeric, pepper, and salt. Simmer 18–20 minutes until water is absorbed. Fluff with fork.
  7. Assemble bowls: start with kale, add quinoa, sweet potatoes, and chickpeas.
  8. Drizzle with Lemon Tahini Herb Sauce and garnish with sunflower seeds and dill.
