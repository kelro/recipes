Probiotic Cultured Drink

A fermented beverage designed to support butyrate-producing gut bacteria.

Beverages Fermented 24–48 hours

Ingredients
Use non-chlorinated water for fermentation.
  • 1 liter filtered water
  • 2 tablespoons inulin powder
  • 1 tablespoon resistant starch (potato starch or green banana flour)
  • 1 capsule Clostridium butyricum probiotic
  • 1 capsule Bifidobacterium infantis probiotic
  • 1 capsule Anaerobutyricum hallii probiotic
  • Optional: 1 capsule Akkermansia muciniphila probiotic
Procedure
Maintain warmth but avoid direct heat.
  1. Pour the filtered water into a clean glass jar.
  2. Whisk in the inulin powder and resistant starch until fully dissolved.
  3. Open each probiotic capsule and add the contents to the jar.
  4. Stir gently to distribute the cultures evenly.
  5. Cover the jar loosely with a breathable cloth or fermentation lid.
  6. Allow to ferment at room temperature for 24 to 48 hours.
  7. Once lightly tangy, refrigerate and consume within 5 days.
Notes: Start with small servings to assess tolerance. This drink is not carbonated and should remain mildly sour, not alcoholic or fizzy.
