Thai Rice Soup

Ingredients
Bright flavors with simple preparation.
  • 1 tablespoon peanut oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carton (32 oz) Swanson® Thai Ginger Flavor Infused Broth
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and cut into 2-inch matchstick-thin strips (about 1 cup)
  • 1/2 of a 16-ounce package wide rice noodles, prepared according to package directions
  • 3 tablespoons sliced green onions
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • 3 tablespoons chopped salted peanuts
  • Lime wedges, for serving
Procedure
Quick cook, best served fresh.
  1. Heat the peanut oil in a 2½-quart saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the garlic and cook, stirring constantly, for about 45 seconds.
  3. Add the broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat to medium. Add the carrot and cook for 5 minutes, or until tender-crisp.
  5. Add the prepared rice noodles and cook until the mixture is hot and bubbling.
  6. Serve topped with green onions, cilantro, and chopped peanuts.
  7. Serve lime wedges on the side.
Notes: This soup is best enjoyed immediately; the noodles will continue to soften if it sits.
