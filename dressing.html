Ingredients
Classic holiday dressing.
  • Pepperidge Farm Herb Seasoning Stuffing
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 stick butter
  • 32 oz chicken broth
  • 1/4 tsp sage
  • 1/4 tsp poultry seasoning
Procedure
Slow-cooked for moist, flavorful dressing.
  1. Sauté onion and celery in butter until tender.
  2. Add sage and poultry seasoning to the sautéed mixture and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Place stuffing into the crockpot.
  4. Pour sautéed mixture over the stuffing.
  5. Add chicken broth and mix well.
  6. Cook on High for 4 hours.
  7. Add additional broth during cooking as needed to keep dressing moist.
