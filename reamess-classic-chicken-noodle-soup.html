Reames® Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Makes ~20 servings (cups)

Ingredients
Classic, hearty, and filling.
  • 1 lb cooked boneless, skinless chicken
  • 12 cups chicken broth
  • 1 package (24 oz) Reames® Homestyle Egg Noodles
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups chopped onion
  • 2 cups chopped celery
  • 2 cups shredded carrots
  • 2 cups heavy cream or half-and-half
  • 8 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sherry (optional)
Procedure
Simmer, thicken, and serve.
  1. Bring chicken broth to a boil with bay leaves.
  2. Add egg noodles and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring often.
  3. Add parsley, thyme, black pepper, onion, celery, and carrots.
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
  5. Meanwhile, melt butter in a skillet. Add flour and cook for 1–2 minutes.
  6. Add cream to the noodle mixture and bring back to a simmer.
  7. Slowly stir in the flour mixture; cook and stir until thickened, about 2 minutes.
  8. Discard bay leaves.
  9. Add chicken and sherry (if using). Stir to incorporate.
  10. Heat through and serve.
Notes: This soup thickens as it sits — add a little extra broth when reheating if needed.
