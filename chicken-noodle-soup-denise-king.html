Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients
Traditional Romanian-style chicken soup.
  • 10–12 chicken wings, washed (or half chicken wings and half thighs)
  • 3 medium yellow onions, quartered
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 3 large carrots, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large or 2 small celery root (optional)
  • 2 tbsp fresh chopped parsley
  • 1 cup soup noodles, cooked al dente
Procedure
Slow simmer for deep, clean flavor.
  1. In a large soup pot, add the chicken wings, onions, salt, and pepper.
  2. Fill the pot with cold water until the chicken is fully covered.
  3. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat and simmer, skimming foam as needed.
  4. Add carrots, celery, and celery root (if using).
  5. Continue simmering until the chicken is fully cooked and vegetables are tender.
  6. Remove chicken pieces; debone if desired and return meat to the pot.
  7. Add cooked soup noodles and chopped parsley.
  8. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot.
Notes: Cooking noodles separately keeps the broth clear and prevents them from becoming too soft.
