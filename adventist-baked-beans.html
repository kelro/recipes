Adventist Baked Beans

A traditional Loma Linda–style baked bean dish.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups dried navy beans
  • 8 cups water (for soaking)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons molasses
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • Salt to taste
Procedure
  1. Soak beans overnight. Drain and cook in fresh water until tender.
  2. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  3. Combine beans with remaining ingredients in a baking dish.
  4. Cover and bake 2–3 hours, stirring occasionally.
