Paella (Chicken & Seafood)

Ingredients
Classic chicken + seafood with saffron rice.
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp saffron threads
  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 1/2 lb chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 lb mussels, scrubbed and debearded
  • 1/4 cup peas (fresh or frozen)
  • Lemon wedges, for serving
Procedure
Build flavor, simmer, rest, serve.
  1. Heat olive oil in a large paella pan or wide, shallow skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, garlic, and red bell pepper; cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in paprika and saffron; cook 1 minute more.
  4. Add rice; stir to coat with oil and spices. Cook 1–2 minutes, until slightly toasted.
  5. Pour in chicken broth and white wine; bring to a simmer.
  6. Add chicken, shrimp, mussels, and peas. Stir gently to combine.
  7. Reduce heat to low and cover. Cook 18–20 minutes, or until rice is tender and seafood is cooked through.
  8. Remove from heat and let sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
  9. Serve with lemon wedges on the side.
Notes: This is a flexible base recipe — adjust proteins/vegetables to taste.
