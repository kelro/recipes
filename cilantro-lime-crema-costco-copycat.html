Cilantro Lime Crema (Costco Copycat)

A creamy, tangy sauce with fresh cilantro and lime, inspired by the Costco favorite.

Ingredients
Creamy base with fresh herbs and citrus
  • 1/2 cup sour cream (regular or light)
  • 1/4–1/2 cup fresh cilantro, to taste
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise (regular or light)
  • 1/2 jalapeño, ribs and seeds removed, chopped
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1/2 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4–1/2 cup milk (optional, for thinning)
Procedure
Blend until smooth and adjust to taste
  1. Place the chopped jalapeño pieces in a small food processor or blender and pulse until finely chopped.
  2. Add the sour cream, cilantro, mayonnaise, lime juice, red wine vinegar, salt, and garlic powder. Blend until smooth.
  3. If a thinner consistency is desired, add milk a little at a time and blend until the sauce reaches your preferred thickness.
  4. Taste and adjust salt or cilantro as needed. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
