Cuban Black Beans

Classic Blue Zones–style Cuban beans

Ingredients
Savory, citrus-forward beans
  • 2 cups dried black beans, soaked overnight
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure
Slow-simmered until tender
  1. Cook soaked beans in fresh water until tender; drain.
  2. Sauté onion and pepper in olive oil until soft.
  3. Add garlic, cumin, oregano, and bay leaves.
  4. Add beans and enough cooking liquid to cover.
  5. Simmer 30 minutes. Stir in vinegar and season.
