Corned Beef

Ingredients
Classic brined brisket with warm spices.
  • 1 (5–6 lb) beef brisket
  • 1 gallon water
  • 2 cups kosher salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup curing salt
  • 2 tbsp mustard seeds
  • 2 tbsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp cloves
  • 1 tbsp allspice berries
  • 1 tbsp juniper berries
  • 6 bay leaves
Procedure
Brine, simmer, rest.
  1. In a large pot, combine water, kosher salt, sugar, curing salt, mustard seeds, peppercorns, cloves, allspice, juniper berries, and bay leaves.
  2. Bring to a boil, stirring until salt and sugar are dissolved. Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature.
  3. Place the brisket in a large container and pour the cooled brine over the meat, ensuring it is fully submerged.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for 5–7 days.
  5. Remove brisket from brine and rinse well under cold water. Discard the brine.
  6. Place brisket in a large pot and cover with fresh water.
  7. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 2½ to 3 hours, until tender.
  8. Remove from pot and let rest for 15 minutes before slicing.
Notes: Slice against the grain for maximum tenderness. Leftovers make excellent sandwiches.
