Baked Cauliflower

Air fryer method

Ingredients
Simple, crisp, and well-seasoned.
  • 1 medium cauliflower (2–3 lb), cut into florets
  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp Old Bay seasoning
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
Procedure
Air fry until golden and tender.
  1. Preheat air fryer to 375°F.
  2. Toss cauliflower florets with olive oil, Old Bay seasoning, kosher salt, and black pepper until evenly coated.
  3. Place cauliflower in the air fryer basket in a single layer (work in batches if needed).
  4. Air fry for 10–15 minutes, flipping every 5 minutes, until golden and tender.
  5. Serve immediately.
