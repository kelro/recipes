Baked White Fish with Lemon-Herb Sauce

Simple baked white fish with a light lemon, garlic, and herb sauce.

Main Dishes Baked ~20 minutes

Ingredients
For the baked white fish
  • 4 white fish fillets (bass, perch, or similar)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
Procedure
Bake until tender
  1. Preheat the oven to 400 °F.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the fish fillets in a baking dish and drizzle evenly with the lemon-herb sauce.
  4. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
Notes: This recipe works well with most mild white fish. Thicker fillets may need an additional few minutes of baking time. Serve with roasted vegetables, rice, or a simple green salad.
