The Best Ever Veggie Enchiladas

Flavorful vegetarian enchiladas with sautéed veggies, black beans, cheese, and enchilada sauce.

Ingredients
For the veggie enchiladas
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 large green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 15 oz black beans, drained
  • 2 tablespoons cream cheese
  • 10 large flour tortillas
  • 1½ cups shredded Mexican cheese
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • Juice of ½ large lime
  • 8 oz enchilada sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: avocado slices, lime wedges
Procedure
Assemble and bake
  1. Combine onion, garlic, green pepper, and olive oil in a large skillet. Sauté over medium-high heat until the onion is soft and translucent (about 6–8 minutes).
  2. Add frozen corn to the skillet and cook for 4–5 minutes until thawed and heated through.
  3. Add drained black beans, cream cheese, ½ cup shredded Mexican cheese, smoked paprika, ground cumin, chili powder, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Stir until the mixture is creamy and well combined.
  4. Warm the tortillas in the microwave until pliable to make rolling easier.
  5. Spoon filling down the center of each tortilla, roll up, and place seam side down in a greased 9×13 baking dish.
  6. Pour enchilada sauce evenly over the rolled enchiladas. Sprinkle with remaining shredded Mexican cheese.
  7. Bake in a preheated 350 °F oven for 20–25 minutes, until cheese is melted and bubbly.
Notes: Warming the tortillas before rolling helps prevent cracking. Top enchiladas with fresh avocado slices and extra lime juice for brightness when serving. Adjust spices to taste.
