Adventist Vegetable Soup

A hearty, plant-forward soup inspired by Seventh-day Adventist traditions.

Ingredients
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 cup tomatoes, chopped or canned
  • 1 cup cooked beans (kidney, pinto, or navy)
  • 6 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure
  1. Sauté onion, garlic, carrots, and celery in a small amount of broth until softened.
  2. Add potatoes, green beans, tomatoes, beans, oregano, and broth.
  3. Bring to a gentle boil, then simmer 30–40 minutes until tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
